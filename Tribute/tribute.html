Don Young

1939 - 2011

Bull Rider, Bull Hauler, Husband, Father, Grandfather

"Why do work that can be put off until tomorrow?" - Don Young

Timeline

  • June 27, 1939 - Donald Young was born in Bassett, Iowa to Glen and Eva Young.
  • 1956 - Graduated from Charles High School
  • 1957 - 1959 - Served in the U.S. Army
  • 1960 - Married Julie Ann Miles in Waterloo, Iowa
  • 1972 - Moved to Uehling, Nebraska
  • 2011 - Died in his farmhouse, surrounded by the family
